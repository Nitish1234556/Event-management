About Us

We are Nitish Choudhary and Anand Patel, students from IIT Ropar, pursuing B.Tech in Mathematics and Computing.

🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is to build a clean and fast Event Management System that allows users to:

  • 📌 Discover upcoming events instantly
  • 📝 Easily participate in events without login
  • 👥 Track participation with smooth UI

💡 Why This Project?

In our college life, managing events is often messy. So we thought: “Why not build a lightweight and effective platform?”
This project helped us improve our skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frontend logic using localStorage.

🚀 What’s Next?

  • ✅ Event creation with form
  • 📩 Email notifications
  • 🗃️ Backend database integration
  • 🔒 Login & authentication

🙌 Our Commitment

We believe that a good UI/UX and functional features are key to digital platforms. That’s why we’ve kept our interface clean, and data handling fast and local for now.
